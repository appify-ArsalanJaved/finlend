Onsite Training

We conduct our onsite training sessions at Executive Offices throughout the United States through our membership with Regus offices, or at a location of your choosing. Our trainer will spend two full days working with you to ensure that you have learned everything you need to know to become a successful financial services provider.

Webinar Training

We also offer a Webinar based training program that lasts between four to six hours. With this option, our corporate trainer will connect with you over an online meeting and show you how to access and utilize our various programs. The advantage of this training option is that it is the least expensive and requires the least amount of time to get up and running. This option will cover the same information as the onsite training.

Technical Support

Our training and support does not stop at the end of the formal training sessions. We will continue to support you as long as you are an active affiliate of FinLend. Our finance experts can help you identify solutions to difficult financing situations, review your transaction documents, offer ongoing advice, and answer all your questions.

As an affiliate, you will be able to provide the financing to your customers and receive a commission in the process.

About Us

In 2020, FinLend evolved from a company that focused solely on providing lending services to its direct customers from several branch locations to a company focused on building a nationwide network of independent lending partners. We provide all the training, guidance, infrastructure and ongoing support necessary to help entrepreneurial individuals start and operate their own finance company.

Because we have a deep understanding of the industry, we've designed our Affiliate program to be straightforward and easy to use. We worked hard to identify the best parts of the industry and we have simplified it into a program that maximizes our affiliates’ opportunities for success. We believe that we can train virtually anyone to learn, follow, and prosper from our Program.

We are constantly identifying and integrating the best lending products into our program so that our Affiliates will have access to them.

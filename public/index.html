An Exciting and Rewarding Profession

In 2020, Affiliated Financial Partners evolved from a company that focused solely on providing lending services to its direct customers from several branch locations to a company focused on building a nationwide network of independent lending partners. We provide all the training, guidance, infrastructure and ongoing support necessary to help entrepreneurial individuals start and operate their own finance company.

Because we have a deep understanding of the industry, we've designed our Affiliate program to be straightforward and easy to use. We worked hard to identify the best parts of the industry and we have simplified it into a program that maximizes our affiliates’ opportunities for success. We believe that we can train virtually anyone to learn, follow, and prosper from our Program.
